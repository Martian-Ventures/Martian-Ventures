Our Services

Enforced Learning Management System (LMS)

This system is designed to train oil and gas industry workers on company policies, safety procedures, and job responsibilities. Features include:

  • Mandatory Training Programs- Ensuring all employees complete essential training before starting work.
  • Compliance and Certification Tracking - Monitoring employee progress and ensuring compliance with industry regulations.
  • Performance Assessments and Reports – Evaluating employee understanding and generating progress reports.
  • Interactive Learning Modules -Using videos, simulations, and quizzes to enhance learning.

SCADA Systems

SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems provide real-time plant intelligence and centralized control for industrial operations. Our SCADA solutions deliver live monitoring, alarm management, operator HMIs, and a data historian for full operational visibility across production sites and facilities.

Key capabilities include Performance Ratio & Yield analytics, historical trending, predictive maintenance, and consolidated dashboards that help teams optimize throughput and reduce downtime. We also provide secure, scalable connectivity to edge devices and support standard industrial protocols (Modbus, OPC UA, DNP3).

  • Real-time Plant Intelligence – Live telemetry, dashboards and KPI monitoring.
  • Performance Ratio & Yield Analytics – Track efficiency, losses and production yield.
  • Integration with EMS, BMS & Weather Stations – Consolidated operational context for better decisions.
  • Alarm Management & Historical Trending – Prioritize events and analyze past performance.
  • Predictive Maintenance & Data Historian – Use trends and ML-ready datasets to forecast failures.
  • Secure, Scalable Connectivity – Edge device support, role-based access and protocol compatibility.
  • Customizable HMI & Reports – Tailored views for operators, managers and analysts.

Predictive Maintenance Solutions

These AI-driven solutions analyze equipment performance to predict failures before they occur. Features include:

  • Sensor-Based Monitoring – Using IoT devices to track machine health
  • AI and Machine Learning Analytics – Predicting potential failures and suggesting maintenance schedules.
  • Automated Alerts and Notifications – Informing engineers when maintenance is needed

Drone Surveillance Systems

Our Drone Surveillance Systems provide aerial monitoring and inspection services for large sites and remote infrastructure. Equipped with high-resolution and thermal cameras, RTK GPS and automated flight planning, drones deliver fast situational awareness and help reduce inspection time and operational risk.

Use cases include perimeter security, pipeline and asset inspections, emergency response support, progress monitoring, and environmental surveys. Video feeds and telemetry can be integrated into SCADA, asset management platforms and command dashboards for centralized monitoring.

  • High-resolution & Thermal Imaging – Detailed visual and temperature inspections.
  • Automated Flight Plans & BVLOS Support – Scheduled missions and beyond-visual-line-of-sight readiness where permitted.
  • Live Video Streaming & Telemetry – Real-time situational awareness for operators.
  • GIS Mapping & Change Detection – Track site changes and generate orthomosaics.
  • Integration with SCADA/Asset Systems – Feed aerial data into existing operational platforms.
  • Regulatory Compliance & Data Security – Flight logging, encrypted storage and secure transfer.
